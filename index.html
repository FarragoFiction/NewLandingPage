SBURBSim

Procedural text and graphic simulations of SBURBSession from the Homestuck webcomic. Read random sessions, or create custom characters to toss into the Game. (Written in dart, so no longer able to be updated by JR).

ZampanioSim

Various attempts to capture the feel of the Zampanio fandom, including rumors and fanhacks.

DollSim

Create your own shareable doll (that can be used in various older Farrago games), in a variety of style (most notably, Homestuck sprites).

WigglerSim

Take the role of a Jadeblooded Troll from homestuck and raise little wigglers all your own, and prepare them for their lives outside of the caverns.

AudioLogs

Want to hear an eclectic mix of audio logs that can be discovered in various other games (or a few through puzzles on the AudioLog page itself?). Go wild.

FridgeSim

Collection of fanart submitted, mostly about SBURBSim!

Farragnarok Char Guide

Curious as to what all this "Farragnarok" stuff is about? Check out the main cast!

LOHAE

First game in the Farragnarok series, help Zawhei Bacama, the Reaper of Life, pick fruits on the Land of Horticulture and Essence.

LOMAT

Second game in the Farragnarok series, help ButlerBot clean up after his fleshy doppelganger, Eirikr Kharun, the Guide of Void. (Note: Last Land we see in the Farragnarok Series, due to the Wasted Guide of Void's Interference).

LOGAC

Game for the Wright of Law, help owl consorts of the Land of Games and Confusion compromise on rules so they can get back to playing!

PaldemicSim

See DMs between various characters and even write your own. Apollo's gift of prophecy struck hard, this was named pre-pandemic.
