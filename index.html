Old Homepage

Here's what the home page used to look like, back when Farrago was trying to go legit.

SBURBSim

Procedural text and graphic simulations of SBURBSession from the Homestuck webcomic. Read random sessions, or create custom characters to toss into the Game. (Written in dart, so no longer able to be updated by JR).

ZampanioSim

Various attempts to capture the feel of the Zampanio fandom, including rumors and fanhacks. Navigate the branches at your own peril. Remember to hydrate and take breaks. Obsession is a dangerous thing. I'd recommend going North, first.

DollSim

Create your own shareable doll (that can be used in various older Farrago games), in a variety of style (most notably, Homestuck sprites).

WigglerSim

Take the role of a Jadeblooded Troll from homestuck and raise little wigglers all your own, and prepare them for their lives outside of the caverns.

Gigglesnort

On a distant Earth, a phone rings. "Thank you for contacting Farrago Fiction Narrative Stability Customer Support. So that I can better assist you, may I please have your session number?"

AudioLogs

Want to hear an eclectic mix of audio logs that can be discovered in various other games (or a few through puzzles on the AudioLog page itself?). Go wild.

Audio Eggs

You can see a list of all the AudioLogs or similar easter eggs you've found.

FridgeSim

Collection of fanart submitted, mostly about SBURBSim!

Farragnarok Char Guide

Curious as to what all this "Farragnarok" stuff is about? Check out the main cast!

LOHAE

First game in the Farragnarok series, help Zawhei Bacama, the Reaper of Life, grow and pick fruits on the Land of Horticulture and Essence. There are definitely no secrets in this zen gardening game.

LOMAT

Second game in the Farragnarok series, help ButlerBot clean up after his fleshy doppelganger, Eirikr Kharun, the Guide of Void. (Note: Last Land we see in the Farragnarok Series, due to the Wasted Guide of Void's Interference).

LOGAC

Game for the Wright of Law, help owl consorts of the Land of Games and Confusion compromise on rules so they can get back to playing!

PaldemicSim

See DMs between various characters and even write your own. Apollo's gift of prophecy struck hard, this was named pre-pandemic.

April ARGS

Link to a collection of past April ARGs. Not up to date.

Magical Girl Sim

I barely remember making this! It...looks like some kind of dress up game where you send magical girls on adventures? Mostly I remember it for spawning MonsterGirlSim for that Ludum Dare....

MonsterGirlSim

Haha yeah, there it is. It was so cool it made MagicalGirlSim be a side page in it's own folder. This was an entry in Ludum Dare. Help the various Magical Girls protect the city without paying too steep a price.

99RoomsSim

Tired of wandering the Backrooms? Settle in for some nice and relaxing picture viewing. Wander far more than 99 rooms as you look for Doors and avoid (or collect) glitches.

HydrationSim

You DO know farragofiction.com isn't the only site I run, didn't you? Remember to stay hydrated. (Runs on the ZampanioSim engine used by my first three branches of Zampanio.)

Garden of the Mind

Runs a bit hot because I didn't know animations very well back then. Practice typing out affirmations and fighting off brain lies in this ACTUAL chill garden game.

LORAS

Haha, that's right, I went and took my land, (The Land of Rods and Screens) and made a version that isn't forced to be a sub bit of SBURBSim anymore. Ah, memories.

ButlerBot

Talk to ButlerBot. He's lonely out there in the void. I hope he's not starting to lose it with so little to do... Or just read his logs and see what other people are saying to him. Unless its his lunchbreak and legally mandated two fifteen minute beaks, of course.

LifeSim

Inspired by the game of life from Problem Sleuth, watch how your life plays out based on what cards you get dealt. In theory you can create your own custom life, but I wildly overestimated how easy to use the tools are.

AlchemySim

Play around with my take on the Alchemy system from homestuck. Collect all the achievements!

GenderSim

Have you ever wondered what gender you are? Why not try the worst possible way to find out? WARNING: DOES NOT END.

AntSim

These are definitely ants and this is definitely simulation them. It is extremely important that you read this. "As with any Farrago product, the true goal of the game is to look inside and discover what the goal is for yourself."

AtticSim

A traditional text based adventure game for reasons that I'm sure are obvious.

PaleSim

Refreshes every five minutes with positive messages.

CardSim

This appears to no longer fully work. It used to generate random trading cards out of DollSim assets. Rest in peace. The Rot comes for us all in the end.

StrifeSim

One of the first things I did with animation and graphics. It appears to no longer fully work. It used to generate little procedural strifes, like from homestuck. You'd start fighting a parent, then move to regular imps, then to a denizen, then a Queen.

Midnight

A story written by manicInsomniac that I gave polish to via web effects.

TurtleSim

The rot takes all in the end. It no longer fully works. It used to render a turtle doll of a random color that you could click on to make it say the 'fuck' word, based on a glitch in SBURBSim. The Turtle doens't render but you can click its ghost and it seems to still work.

SBURBSim Classic

SBURBSim as it existed very earily in it's life, written in pure javascript instead of dart and missing many features. (AB is blazing fast tho cuz no AiEngine).

SBURBSim 1.0

SBURBSim in its very first form, no graphics, extremely barebones.

Rigid Heddle Sim

Look, we all got pandemic hobbies. This helped me learn to weave.

CloudSim

KR made this! Watch chill clouds float by synced to time of day.
